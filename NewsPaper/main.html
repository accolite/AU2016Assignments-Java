ON A NEW YATRA

In 1992, soon after finishing school at St Columba’s in Delhi, Dhruv Shringi started a firm that was into chemical manufacturing. But that did not quite take off, and Shringi shut it down two years later and decided to focus on chartered accountancy. He did his CA and while doing so, worked at accounting firm Arthur Andersen. In 2000, he quit Arthur Andersen to do an MBA and then went on to join Ford, and then, about two years later, ebookers in the US. ebookers was an online flight booking service and Shringi was director of group operations and technology.
